Overview

Purpose

Build revenue by helping people find our service and then convince them to buy rafting trips.

Audience

Who is the target audience? Likely customers are: males, ages 14-40, lower-middle class to upper-middle class, adventurous, active, etc. The audience will likely access my website by using their mobile devices to search for activities to do during the summer (most likely on weekends or on a vacation). The audience will want to know location, what is included (guides, life jackets, helmets, transportation from the end of the trip back to the parking lot, etc), prices, how to book a trip, available dates and times, etc.
