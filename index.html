Idaho, USA

Idaho is known for potatoes and the great outdoors. It is in the North-West of the United States and borders Canada. Home to a part of Yellowstone National Park, Lucky Peak State Park, Thousand Springs State Park, Bruneau Dunes State Park, Nez Perce National Historical Park, Shoshone Falls Park, Minidoka National Historic Site, Hells Canyon National Recreation Area, Farragut State Park, Hagerman Fossil Beds National Monument, City of Rocks National Reserve, Craters of the Moon National Monument, Targhee National Forrest, etc. Ben Wyatt on Parks and Rec was right when he said, "Idaho is basically one big park." There are many people who enjoy camping, fishing, rafting, kayaking, boating, hunting, skiing, snowboarding, hiking, rock climbing, etc in the Gem State.
